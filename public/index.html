Maple runs free, right on your device

Maple's AI runs entirely inside your browser — no account, no API key, nothing to pay, and your conversations never leave your computer.

The first time, your browser downloads the AI model once (a few hundred MB to ~1 GB). After that it loads instantly and even works offline.
