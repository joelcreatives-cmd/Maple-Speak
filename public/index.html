Maple runs free, right on your device

Maple's AI runs entirely inside your browser — no account, no API key, nothing to pay, and your conversations never leave your device.

The first time, your browser downloads the AI model once (a few hundred MB to ~1 GB). After that it loads instantly and even works offline.

This device or browser can't run the in-browser AI yet because it doesn't support WebGPU. Try opening Maple Speak in an up-to-date Chrome or Edge — on Android, or on a Windows, macOS, or Linux machine. On iPhone or iPad, update to the latest iOS and try again.
